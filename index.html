Welcome!

(un)official Team Photo

This is the official site of Team Enginuity. We are Warwick High School's Robotics Team in Newport News, Virginia. This year (the 2013-2014 school year) is our second. We are glad that you have decided to take the time to come check out our website! Whether you are part of a veteran FIRST team, a rookie team or not part of a team, our site contains lots of useful information. Here, you can find current information about the 2013 FTC Game, Block Party; and the 2014 FRC Game, Aerial Assist; along with further information about our team and our sponsors, media from previous competitons, and resources for other teams.
